## Overview

The polyphonic editing capability in <a href="#">Melodyne Studio</a> is most powerfully demonstrated in the correction of recorded piano, guitar, and other harmonic instruments where individual notes within chords are out of tune or poorly timed. In a recorded piano chord where one note was played slightly flat, traditional tools can only adjust the entire chord uniformly — raising all notes would improve the flat note but push the in-tune notes sharp. <a href="#">Melodyne's DNA technology</a> addresses only the individual out-of-tune note within the chord, leaving the other simultaneously sounding notes entirely unchanged. This note-specific precision is what makes truly corrective editing of polyphonic material possible for the first time.

The formant processing capabilities in <a href="#">Melodyne Studio</a> are essential for natural-sounding pitch correction results, particularly for vocal editing. When pitch is corrected without formant adjustment, the vocal quality changes in a way that sounds unnatural — higher pitches sound thin and pinched, lower pitches sound larger than the natural voice. Melodyne's independent formant control maintains the vocal character regardless of pitch movement, producing corrections that preserve the natural timbre and character of the performance while correcting the pitch to the desired target. This formant independence is also a creative tool, enabling <a href="#">voice character modification</a> — creating the impression of a larger or smaller vocal instrument — as a distinct creative parameter.

The creative applications of <a href="#">Melodyne Studio</a> extend well beyond correction into territory that represents genuinely new musical possibilities. Reharmonizing existing audio by changing the notes of a recorded chord progression, extracting MIDI note data from a recorded audio performance for arrangement and orchestration purposes, creating new melodic variations from existing recorded material, and separating the harmonic content of a complex mix for creative remixing are all workflows that Melodyne's technology makes feasible. The <a href="#">time-stretching quality</a> at extreme ratios is exceptional, making it practical to use Melodyne for tempo change applications where other tools produce artifacts that limit usable range. For audio professionals who work with complex material and require the most capable pitch and time editing tools available, <a href="#">CelemonyMelodyneStudio OSX</a> provides capabilities that no other application matches.
